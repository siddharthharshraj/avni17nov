# Improving Maternal & Child Health Outcomes

## The Challenge

Calcutta Kids works in urban slums providing maternal and child health services. Managing health records, tracking pregnancies, and monitoring child development for thousands of beneficiaries was challenging:

- **Paper-based records** difficult to maintain
- **Tracking high-risk cases** manually
- **Limited visibility** into program performance
- **Difficulty in follow-ups** for missed appointments

## The Solution

Avni provided Calcutta Kids with:

- **Digital health records** for mothers and children
- **Automated risk stratification** for high-risk pregnancies
- **Appointment reminders** and follow-up tracking
- **Real-time reporting** on program indicators

## The Impact

Results achieved:

- **15,000+ beneficiaries** tracked digitally
- **35% improvement** in antenatal checkup compliance
- **Early detection** of 90% high-risk pregnancies
- **Reduced child malnutrition** by 28%

## Key Features

1. Beneficiary registration and profiling
2. Pregnancy tracking with risk assessment
3. Child growth monitoring
4. Immunization scheduling
5. Home visit tracking
