# Digital Healthcare in Rural India

## The Challenge

Managing maternal and child health programs across 500+ remote villages required:

- Tracking thousands of beneficiaries
- Scheduling regular checkups
- Monitoring high-risk cases
- Coordinating with ASHA workers

## The Solution

Avni provided:

- **Mobile health records** accessible offline
- **Automated reminders** for checkups
- **Risk stratification** for priority cases
- **Real-time reporting** to health authorities

## The Impact

- **50,000+ beneficiaries** tracked digitally
- **40% improvement** in checkup compliance
- **Early identification** of high-risk pregnancies
- **Reduced infant mortality** by 25%
