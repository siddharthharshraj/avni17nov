# Scaling Water & Sanitation Programs With Real-Time Data

## The Challenge

Arghyam works to ensure safe, sustainable water for all. Managing water quality monitoring, sanitation facility tracking, and community engagement across multiple states required:

- **Real-time water quality data** from remote locations
- **Tracking sanitation facilities** and maintenance schedules
- **Community participation** monitoring
- **Impact measurement** across diverse geographies

## The Solution

Avni enabled Arghyam to:

- **Digital water quality testing** with offline data collection
- **Facility tracking** with GPS coordinates and photos
- **Community engagement** monitoring and feedback
- **Real-time dashboards** for program managers

## The Impact

Since implementing Avni:

- **1,000+ water sources** monitored digitally
- **50% reduction** in response time for maintenance
- **Real-time visibility** of water quality across regions
- **Improved community engagement** with 80% participation rate

## Key Features Used

1. Offline data collection for remote areas
2. GPS tracking for water sources
3. Photo documentation of facilities
4. Custom dashboards for monitoring
5. Community feedback integration
