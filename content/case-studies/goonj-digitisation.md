# How Goonj Uses Avni To Digitise Offline Data Collection And Inventory Flow

## The Challenge

Goonj's work is guided by the principle of dignity over charity. Material is never simply given away; it is received as recognition of a community's effort in addressing its own needs. This philosophy influences how they approach data collection and inventory management.

Operating across multiple states with thousands of volunteers, Goonj faced significant challenges:

- **Manual inventory tracking** across 20+ collection centers
- **Offline data collection** in remote areas with no internet connectivity
- **Complex material flow** from urban collection to rural distribution
- **Limited visibility** into real-time inventory status

## The Solution

Avni provided Goonj with a comprehensive digital platform that:

### 1. Offline-First Data Collection
Field workers can collect data without internet connectivity, with automatic sync when online.

### 2. Real-Time Inventory Tracking
Track material flow from collection to sorting to distribution across all centers.

### 3. Mobile-First Design
Simple, intuitive interface that works on basic Android devices.

### 4. Custom Workflows
Tailored processes matching Goonj's unique dignity-based distribution model.

## The Impact

Since implementing Avni, Goonj has achieved:

- **70% reduction** in data entry time
- **Real-time visibility** across 20+ collection centers
- **100% offline capability** for field workers
- **Improved accuracy** in inventory tracking
- **Faster decision-making** with live dashboards

## Key Features Used

1. **Offline Data Sync** - Works seamlessly in areas with poor connectivity
2. **Custom Forms** - Tailored to Goonj's specific material categories
3. **Photo Documentation** - Visual tracking of material condition
4. **Multi-level Approval** - Workflow matching organizational hierarchy
5. **Dashboard & Reports** - Real-time insights for management
