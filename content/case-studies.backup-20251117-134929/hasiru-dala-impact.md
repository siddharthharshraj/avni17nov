# Empowering Waste Pickers Through Tech

## The Challenge

Hasiru Dala works with waste pickers across Karnataka, providing them with dignity, identity, and livelihood opportunities. Managing data for thousands of waste pickers manually was:

- Time-consuming and error-prone
- Difficult to track individual progress
- Hard to measure impact at scale
- Challenging to provide timely support

## The Solution

Avni enabled Hasiru Dala to:

- **Digital Identity Cards** for all waste pickers
- **Track earnings** and work patterns
- **Monitor health** and welfare needs
- **Measure impact** with real-time data

## The Impact

- **5,000+ waste pickers** registered and tracked
- **30% increase** in average earnings
- **Real-time monitoring** of welfare programs
- **Data-driven decisions** for program expansion

## Key Features

1. Beneficiary tracking and profiling
2. Earnings and attendance monitoring
3. Health checkup scheduling
4. Impact measurement dashboards
