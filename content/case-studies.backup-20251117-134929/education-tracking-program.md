# Tracking Student Progress & Learning Outcomes

## The Challenge

An education-focused NGO working across 200 schools needed to:

- **Track individual student progress** over multiple years
- **Monitor learning outcomes** across different subjects
- **Identify at-risk students** early
- **Measure program effectiveness** with data

## The Solution

Avni enabled the organization to:

- **Digital student profiles** with learning history
- **Assessment tracking** with automatic scoring
- **Early warning system** for at-risk students
- **Teacher performance** monitoring
- **Parent engagement** tracking

## The Impact

Achievements:

- **10,000+ students** tracked digitally
- **45% improvement** in learning outcomes
- **Early identification** of struggling students
- **80% increase** in parent engagement
- **Data-driven** teaching interventions

## Key Features Used

1. Student enrollment and profiling
2. Assessment and grade tracking
3. Attendance monitoring
4. Teacher observation forms
5. Parent-teacher meeting tracking
6. Learning outcome dashboards

## Success Story

The program identified 500+ students at risk of dropping out and provided targeted interventions, resulting in 85% retention rate.
